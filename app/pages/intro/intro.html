Adjusting gain before you record

Use the gain control slider (1) to ensure sound levels are OK. The % peaks indicator (2) is computed by repeatedly calculating the peak volume; it shows the percent of all computed volume peaks that have reached the overall max volume thus far. (2) can be reset by clicking it, which also resets the "max volume thus far" indicator (3).

Record Page (Cont'd)

Use the gain control slider (5) to ensure volume levels are OK before recording. Adjust (5) to ensure the % peaks indicator (6) -- which indicates the percent of volume peaks, out of all peaks measured, that have reached max volume -- remains a low number (clicking on (6) restarts all stats). The max volume indicator (2) should also be close to the right.
