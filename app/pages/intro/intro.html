Using the Record Page

The record page continuously monitors your microphone's volume level at the top row - the volume indicator. In it, the thin white rectangular border shows the highest volume recorded (max volume). To start recording hit the red record button. Pause or resume the recording with the same button. To stop recording and save the audio to a file, hit the stop button - an audio file is then automatically saved in your library's Unfiled folder under the filename of today's date and time. To navigate to the library page use the navigation side menu. The display at the top-right shows the percentage of all volume peaks recorded that is at the current maximum. The top-right display is also a reset button - clicking on it resets the max-volume indicator and the percent peaks at max and restarts their measurement.
