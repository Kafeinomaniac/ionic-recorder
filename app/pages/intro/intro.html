Using the Record Page

The record page continuously monitors the volume levels of your microphone. The max volume is shown by the thin white rectangle in the volume indicator. To start recording hit the red record button. The same button is used to pause a recording once it has started. To stop a recording and save it to a file, hit the stop button - an audio file is then automatically saved in your library's Unfiled folder under the filename of today's date and time. To navigate to the library page use the navigation side menu.
