Using the Record Page

The volume indicator (1) continuously monitors your microphone's' volume. In it, the thin white rectangule (2) shows the max-volume thus far. Hit the record button (3) to start, pause or resume recording. Hit (4) to stop, at which point the recorded file gets saved in your library's Unfiled folder whose filename is the current time.

Record Page (Cont'd)

To navigate to the library page use the side menu (5). The top right (6) shows the percentage of all volume peaks that have reached the max-volume - click on it to reset peak volume stats.
